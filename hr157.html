Fish positions are few and include: Naiant (swimming/fesswise), Haurient (vertical/palewise), Urinant (haurient inverted),
embowed (arched/leaping), erect (for sea-beasts: animal upper half rampant, lower fish-tail half curled). That's about it.
Dolphins (not natural, but the heraldic dolphins) should be embowed "S" fashion. Fish only arch or are straight).
Any evolving should be on the part of the submittor. (G)
