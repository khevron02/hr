Heraldrydiculous

by Khevron

Trees can be shown as whole trees in many varied types (Oak, Ash, Rowan, Willow, Pine etc), with fruit "fructed" (the fruit of which is depicted unrealistically large or without fruit, Blasted (no leaves), Eradicated (showing the root system), couped (Cut off straight at the ground Stumps or "stocks", Trunks, or in a group of trees called a "hurst".
There are also branches, fruit or nuts shown by themselves, leaved and or slipped etc.
