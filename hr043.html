A "Ford" is a base barry wavy azure and argent, or if on a colored field, argent and azure for contrast.
It represents water or a river.

A Charge, is any item place on armory other than an ordinary. Charges and other items on a shield are layered. In Service of the Dream & Heraldic Display!
